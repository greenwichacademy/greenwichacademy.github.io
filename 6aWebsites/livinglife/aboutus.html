About Us

We make clothing for teenager and young adult girls and woman. We have trendy and stylish clothing that is very comfortable. Our clothing can be weared for a casual night out to a dinner with the family.

This idea was thought of in December of 2016, and will be launched around Summer of 2017. Meena Behringer, CEO, President, and Creator of Living Life, has been working with a team of designers, programmers and store owners in order to bring you the full Living Life experience. She is working on proclaiming her destiny with Living Life and hopes that you will join her on the way
