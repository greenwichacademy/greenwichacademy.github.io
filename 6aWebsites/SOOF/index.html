About Us

Our goal is to provide our consumers with the footwear that will not only better lives, but the future of footwear fashion.

When making our products we vow to use the most eco-friendly materials and stand by our strong belief of wildlife preservation. We are believe in vegan production and respect the lives of other living organisms.

In terms of the fuel we use to power our shoes, there is none. S.O.O.Fs are powered by external thermal energy emitted off the body. We have specially engineered our shoes to use this energy and to not release smoke of any kind; sticking to our promise of eco-freindliness
