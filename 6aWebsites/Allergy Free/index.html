About Us

Allergy Free is the pet shop anyone can come into, no matter what they are allergic to. Everyone who owns a pet, from a fish to a dog, will have to walk into a pet store at least once to buy food, toys, etc., however many people are allergic to animals that shed.

We provide a way to make your trip sneeze-free, cough-free, and all around Allergy Free.
