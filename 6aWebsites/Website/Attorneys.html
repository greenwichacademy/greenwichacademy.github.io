Founder & Lead Practitioner

Erin Spiess Chang

Erin Spiess Chang represents real estate developers and investors in connection with a broad range of real estate transactions. Erin approaches each project from the client’s perspective, both in structuring the transaction and in executing the client’s acquisition/development/financing/marketing plan. She helps clients from the early stages of a project, including acquisition, government approvals and subsidy, through financing and development. She negotiates purchase and development agreements, assists with due diligence and financing, and helps structure, negotiate and draft Operating Easement Agreements. Erin is also an active community member, serving as a Board Member of the Junior League of Greenwich with over 15 oyears of experience, Co-Chair of Arch Street Teen Center with two years experience, former PTA President of a local elementarty school, spokesperson for elementary school parents to the Board of Education, manager of a travel hockey program, as well as a community leader in Watch Hill, RI.
