About

The Law Office of Erin Spiess Chang LLC is led by primary practitioner, Erin Spiess Chang, and represents clients in all varieties of residential and commercial real estate transactions, including the purchasing and selling of real estate, and real estate litigation matters. Erin has been engaged in the private practice of law for over 20 years, with a focus on matters in Connecticut and New York State.
