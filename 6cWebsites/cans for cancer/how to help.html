How to Help
            

how to help

Cans for Cancer LOVES it when we have help. We have many fundraisers and a yearly recycle-a-thon. We love to help everyone and we need volunteers to ensure everyone gets there recycling to where it should be as well as making sure money gets to the correct patient. To participate please call 203-115-4682 or email help@cansforcancer.org we are so grateful for your support. If you are unable or uninterested in participate any of these activities you may send recycling or a donation to 222 grey-sloan, ave greenwich, CT. We appreciate everything you do, send us an email to be added to our email list.
