The HoverGolf!

Do you want a way to cruise around the golf course in style? Look no further, because the HoverGolf will answer all of your questions!

The HoverGolf is a way to play golf in style. Reaching your ball is no problem because you don’t even have to drive. The HoverGolf has a GPS that locates your ball for you. All you have to do is scan your ball at the beginning of a round. Some of the amazing features included in a HoverGolf are a comfortable seat, a cooler, windows for optimal viewing pleasure, and powerful engines that can propel the HoverGolf over 70 feet in the air. Also, the HoverGolf has air conditioning or heating, whatever suits your climate. The HoverGolf is an amazing invention that should become a part of every person’s golf round.
