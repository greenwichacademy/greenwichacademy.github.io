Attitude NYC Dance

Sofia Elizabeth Fernandez created Attitude NYC Dance for kids to learn how to dance and to learn the history behind the dance moves, type of dance, and what was going on at that time in history.

This program offers six type of dance, Tap, Jazz, Ballet, Hip-Hop, modern, and contemporary. This program is open to fids from third grade to twelfth grade. We hope you will like to join our program.All clases are opened to everyone and each parent of a student will get updates about new clases and teaching jobs.
