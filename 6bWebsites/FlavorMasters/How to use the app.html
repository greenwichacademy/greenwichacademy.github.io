FlavorMasters

FlavorMasters is an app that you can get download on apple apps. To use it you can choose a flavor by typing it into the search bar and the app will give you all good tasting possible combinations and when you choose a combo like "Maple and Bacon" it will give you a few recipes featuring those particular flavors

With this app we can change the way people cook all over the world, making it a more enjoyable experience.
