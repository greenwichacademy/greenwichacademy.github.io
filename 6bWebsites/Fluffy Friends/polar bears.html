Polar Bears

Sadly, Polar Bears are one of the many animals struggling to survive due to Global Warming. The ice in their habitats is melting leaving them to drift away from their families and swim very often. Fortunately, Polar Bears can swim, but they can't swim forever! We must help them!!!!
