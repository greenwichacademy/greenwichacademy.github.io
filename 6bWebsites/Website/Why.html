Why make it?

Holo Phone was made for multiple causes. First and foremost, it is waterproof, allowing you to use it in the bath, shower, swimming pool and more! Now you can update your instagram wherever you are!

Additionally, there is no chance of dropping your phone and having to pay around $100 for a new screen if you have a Holo Phone!
