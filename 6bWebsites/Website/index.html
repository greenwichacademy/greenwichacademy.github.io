What is it?

Holo Phone is a convenient form of a smartphone. You wear a colorful wristband, and when you double tap on it, a phone is projected onto your forearm and you can use it like a normal phone.

You can customize the wristband by changing the size, color and style. The actual phone is also customizable to fit your arm however you want it to.
