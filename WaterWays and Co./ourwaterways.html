Our WaterWays

Our WaterWays provide fun and easy transportation across the seven continents. They are open to many different ages, and provide the fun of an aquarium; but also provide an easy way to go on vacation.

We also provide family and pet friendly hotels every 50 miles, in addition to stops along the way, that lead to islands and continents.
